Our mission

Standing together for the animals who can't speak for themselves

Report Animal Harm is a place to stand together for the animals who cannot speak for themselves.

This map belongs to everyone. Whether you're a family wanting to keep your pets safe, a rescue working on the front line, someone who has witnessed cruelty, or someone living with its aftermath — your experience matters here, and you are not alone in it. Every report joins a wider community of people who refuse to look away. Reports appear on the map anonymously, so you can contribute safely, without your name attached.

Together, we are building an honest, shared record of harm across Aotearoa — so that no incident goes unseen, no animal is forgotten, and the case for change grows stronger with every voice added.

Report Animal Harm is a record-keeping tool, not an enforcement or investigation service. We gather and map data; we don't attend incidents, collect physical evidence, or take forensic action. What we offer is the bigger picture — a body of evidence that communities, rescues, and advocates can draw on when data matters most.

"Standing together for the animals who can't speak for themselves."
