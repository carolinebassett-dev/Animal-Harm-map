Report trapping activity

Help map where animal trapping is happening across Aotearoa. Your report shows only an approximate area, and every report is reviewed before it appears.

Before you submit

  • Report the general area only — suburb and region. Never an exact street number.
  • Do not name or identify any individual person. This map is about where and how much, not who.
  • Stick to what you've genuinely seen or can point to. A source link helps.
  • Reports are reviewed before publishing and appear as an approximate area on the map.
