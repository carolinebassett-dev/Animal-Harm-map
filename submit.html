Report an incident

Help build the picture of intentional animal harm across Aotearoa. Every report is reviewed before it appears on the map — your contribution helps keep companion animals safe and supports the case for change.

Before you submit

  • Only submit incidents you genuinely believe to be true.
  • Do not name or identify any individual person — describe the incident, the animal, and the location only.
  • Include a source link where you can, to strengthen the report.
  • Reports are reviewed before publishing and appear as an anonymous, colour-coded pin.
